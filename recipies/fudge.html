Fudge

Description

This low-calorie fudge is everything you would expect in a fudge â€” it's creamy and chocolaty! It's made with low-fat condensed milk which lightens up this sweet treat.

Ingrediants

  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 2/3 cup low-fat sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Combine chocolate chips and condensed milk in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat; stir gently until chips are melted. Stir in vanilla and salt, then pour mixture into an ungreased 10-inch square pan.
  2. Place in the refrigerator and chill until set, about 3 hours.
  3. Cut chilled fudge into 25 squares.
